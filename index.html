Ignis AI Bot Features

Wallet Tracking

The platform should be able to track buys/sells from the provided wallets, facilitating seamless monitoring of transactions.

Auto Buying

Enable automated buys on the specified wallets, streamlining the trading process for users.

Customized Limits

Allow users to set personalized buy/sell limits, giving them control over their trading strategies.

Mirror Trading

Provide an option for mirrored trading, where the platform automatically replicates transactions from a tracking wallet to the user's account.

Long-Term Holding

Include an option for users to choose long-term holding, ensuring flexibility in their investment approach.

Multi-Wallet Integration

Support tracking multiple wallets, ensuring that a purchased coin from any of the provided wallets triggers the corresponding action.

Alpha Signal Integration

Enable users to set buy/sell actions based on alpha signals provided by the bot, enhancing decision-making capabilities.

Shitcoin Trading

Incorporate functionality for trading shitcoins profitably, providing diversified trading opportunities.
